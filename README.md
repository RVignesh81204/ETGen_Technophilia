# ET Story OS

## AI-Native Business News Intelligence System

ET Story OS transforms traditional business news into a **living, interactive intelligence experience**.

Instead of reading multiple static articles, users explore a single story through:

- AI-generated briefings
- Role-based insights
- Interactive story graphs
- Audio + video summaries
- Real-time story evolution

---

## Problem

Business news today is:

- Fragmented across multiple articles
- Time-consuming
- Static and non-personalized
- Not optimized for decision-making

Users typically read **5–8 articles** to understand a single story.

---

## Solution — ET Story OS

ET Story OS converts any news topic into a **dynamic intelligence system**.

One story → One complete intelligence experience

### AI Story Intelligence

## System Flow

1. User lands on **Landing Page**
2. Navigates to **Home Page with Hot News**
3. Selects a story or searches topic
4. AI system generates structured story intelligence
5. Role-based insights applied dynamically
6. Graph + timeline rendered
7. User interacts via:
   - Chat
   - Role switching
   - Audio/video playback
8. Story evolves with updates
